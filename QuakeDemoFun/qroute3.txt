1. About
========

QRoute3 is part of a family of utilities I've written over the years for
visualizing Quake demos. This latest version runs in Windows.


2. Planned Features
===================
- Sound playback
- Velocity/Angles display
- Dead enemy removal
- Many configurable options
- Marathon playback?
